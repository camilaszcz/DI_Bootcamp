Queen Elizabeth


II

Elizabeth Alexandra Mary from the House of Windsor, Supreme Governor of the Church of England, Mother of Corgis, World Colonizer and Former Queen of England.

Fun Facts


  1. She was the longest reigning monarch ever in Britain. Beating even Queen Victoria (her great-great-grandma!).
  2. As Queen, she was served by 15 UK Prime Ministers. Including Winston Churchill, Margaret Thatcher and more recently, Boris Johnson.
  3. Her favourite dogs were corgis. She even invented a new breed of dog when her corgi mated with a dachshund belonging to her sister, Princess Margaret, creating the “dorgi”.
  4. She was the only person in the UK allowed to drive without a licence - And she kept driving even in her later years!
  5. Queen Elizabeth II had two birthdays: Her actual birthday 21 April and her official birthday, which was held on a Saturday in June when the weather’s better!
